RULES

MATCH SETTINGS

1.Match Type :- STANDARD
2.Match Duration :- 6min (Default)
3.Injuries :- ON (Default)
4.Extra-Time :- OFF (*ON in Knockouts)
5.Penalties :- OFF (*ON in Knockouts)
6.Substitutions :- 5 (Default)(+1 in Extra-Time)
7.Interval :- 5 (Default)
8.Player Condition :- Random (Both)
9.Deadlines :- 12:00am - 11:59pm (24 Hrs Only)

TIME RULES

-If Match disconnects Between 0-10 Game minutes, then players can start a fresh match again

-If Match disconnects Between 11-45 Game minutes, then play the remaining time left with +3min Additional.

-If Match disconnects Between 46-90 Game minutes, then play the remaining time left with +4min Additional.

CLAIM RULES

-Continous Backpass is strictly prohibited
(LB-CB-DMF-RB-GK). If opposition can provide video proof then rematch will be played. Without Video proof no claim will be given to anyone.

-Both the players should be responsible for their match completion. if someone provide Screenshot of opponent text that he/she is ignoring then the player will be awarded W/O.(*Screenshot after deadline will not be accepted strictly)

-WalkOver will be 1-0 win and W/O Goals will not be counted in Golden Boot Award.

-If Opponent left in between the match after trailing goals and denied to play remaining the opposition will get goals according to below example:- (Suppose 20:00 played [Player 1 (2-0) Player 2], If Player 2 denies to play remaining the as per calculation Player 1 will get 9-0 win)

OTHER RULES

-Time will be strictly maintained no extension in deadlines. If any Matches left after deadline then it will be 0-0. And in case of KO matches if deadline extends then the player with better stats and positon will be taken forward for further rounds. Screenshots counter will be valid within deadline only. No Screenshots will be considered after deadline.

-6 Groups will be Divided with 4 players each. Top 2 Players from each group followed by the best 4 Third Positions will get the Qualification into eFOOTBALL MASTERS TROPHY Round of 16.

-Mis-Behaving with other players is stictly prohibited. {Punishment:- Yellow Card(for little misbehave) Red Card(for using slangs and will be kicked out of the tournament.)}

-All the Participant will meet each other at the group stage once.
